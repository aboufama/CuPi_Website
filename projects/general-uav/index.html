General UAV

A versatile aerial platform designed for multiple applications including reconnaissance, payload delivery, and autonomous navigation.

CuPi

Our General UAV represents a comprehensive approach to aerial robotics, combining advanced flight control systems with modular design principles. The platform is engineered for adaptability across diverse mission profiles, from reconnaissance and surveillance to payload delivery and autonomous navigation.

The system features redundant sensing capabilities, intelligent path planning algorithms, and a robust communication framework that enables reliable operation in challenging environments. The modular payload architecture allows for rapid reconfiguration based on mission requirements.

Advanced flight control systems ensure stable operation even under adverse weather conditions, while the modular design philosophy enables easy maintenance and field servicing.
